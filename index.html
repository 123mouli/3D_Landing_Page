RESUME TRACKER

An AI Resume Tracker is a smart software tool designed to automatically analyze, organize, and track resumes submitted during the recruitment process. It uses artificial intelligence (AI) and natural language processing (NLP) to scan resumes, extract key information (such as skills, experience, education, and achievements), and match candidates against job requirements.
